About badalta.bihar

Bihar ko naye nazar se dekhne ka platform

Est. 2023 🧡

Who We Are

badalta.bihar ek youth-driven digital media platform hai jo Bihar ke asli heroes, real progress, aur vibrant culture ko duniya ke saamne laata hai.

Hum ek chote se idea se shuru hue the – ki Bihar sirf flood, crime aur poverty se nahi jaana jata. Yahan se Chanakya nikle, Aryabhatt nikle, aur aaj bhi lakhs of talented youngsters uth ke duniya ko badal rahe hain.

Hamara team young journalists, content creators aur storytellers se bana hai jo Bihar se hain, Bihar ke liye kaam karte hain.

🎯

Hamara Mission

Bihar ke baare mein logon ki sochne ki tarika badalna. Hum negativity ke badle progress dikhate hain – real stories of real people who are changing Bihar from within.

🔭

Hamari Vision

Ek aisa Bihar jahan ke youngsters apni state pe proud feel karein. Jahan Bihar ki image sirf "backward" se hatakar "innovative, cultural, and progressive" bane.

💎

Hamare Values

  • ✓ Sach aur transparency
  • ✓ Youth empowerment
  • ✓ Bihar ki positivity
  • ✓ Culture ka samman
